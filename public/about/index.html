About

My research interests include Bayesian clustering applied to ‘omics data to extract biological information. Within ‘omics we have huge quantities of data; interpreting this and creating a coherent story of disease is a non-tirivial problem. My aim is to use Bayesian clustering methods (particularly non-parametric methods), specifically in the area of immunology as part of my work with Chris Wallace, to help contribute to the understanding of disease.
